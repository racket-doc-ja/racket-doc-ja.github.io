english

thoughtstem

 (require english) package: english

A utility library for generating English text – e.g. generating plurals and other grammatical annoyances.

procedure

(english s ...) → string?

  s : string?
Just combines all the s values together with a space in between.

Also a good place to do post-processing and cleanups that can easliy be expressed as a regexp-replace. For example, spaces before commas and semi-colons are automatically deleted.

procedure

(plural s) → string?

  s : string?
Takes a string and attempts to make an English plural out of it, if it is not already pluralized.

It uses some pretty dumb algorithms at the moment. This could be improved.

procedure

(a/an s) → string?

  s : string?
Takes a string and adds the appropriate article onto it – depending on whether it begins with a vowel.

procedure

(list-of [#:or word-for-none] s ...) → string?

  word-for-none : "none" = string?
  s : string?
Takes a list and joins them with commas (including the oxford comma). Handles lists of zero or more. You may supply the word-for-none argument to configure what happens when there are zero things.

procedure

(itemize s ...) → string?

  s : string?
Takes a list of things and renders them as an item list, like: 1) first thing; 2) second thing; 3) another thing.

Semi-colons are used as separators. Numbering (of course) begins at 1.
