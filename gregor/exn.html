18 Exceptions

struct

(struct exn:gregor exn:fail ())

The super-type of all Gregor exceptions.

struct

(struct exn:gregor:invalid-offset exn:gregor ())

Raised by resolve-offset/raise when trying to construct a moment that falls into a gap or overlap in a local time-line. See Resolving UTC Offsets for details.

struct

(struct exn:gregor:invalid-pattern exn:gregor ())

Raised by formatting and parsing functions when they are given an invalid pattern.

struct

(struct exn:gregor:parse exn:gregor ())

Raised by parsing functions when they are unable to parse the input with the given pattern.
