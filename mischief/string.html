6.2 mischief/string: Strings

 (require mischief/string) package: mischief

procedure

(string-lines str) → (listof string?)

  str : string?
Splits str into lines.

Example:
> (string-lines "The cat\nin the hat\ncame back.\n")

'("The cat" "in the hat" "came back.")
