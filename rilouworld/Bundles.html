4.1 Rilouw Bundle

 (require rilouworld/bundles/rilouw) package: rilouworld

This bundle is the official bundle for Rilouworld’s main adventure: The World of Ma.

It provides plateform physics, monsters, puzzles, medieval weapons, potions, magic spells...

4.2 Spacecat Bundle

 (require rilouworld/bundles/spacecat)
  package: rilouworld

This bundle provides the bare minimum to make a side scrolling shooter world.
