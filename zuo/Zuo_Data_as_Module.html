6 Zuo Data as Module

 #lang zuo/datum

A module in the zuo/datum language “exports” its content as a list of S-expressions. The export is not a provide in the sense of the zuo language. Instead, the module’s representation (see Zuo Module Protocol) is just a hash table mapping 'datums to the list of S-expressions.
