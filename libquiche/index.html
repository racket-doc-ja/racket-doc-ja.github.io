Libquiche - Miscellaneous Racket libraries

parnikkapore

 (require libquiche) package: libquiche

This collection holds functions and other stuff I wrote to complement the Racket standard library which aren’t significant enough to warrant its own package. Just like a quiche, it’s a mixture of multiple delicious things, all in a unified enclosure of crunchy crust and mellow cheese.

(require libquiche) requires every part of this library.
