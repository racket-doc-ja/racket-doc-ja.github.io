DirName

Maciej Barć <xgqt@riseup.net>

 (require dirname) package: dirname

procedure

(dirnames pth) → string?

  pth : (listof path-string?)

Example:
> (dirnames "/home/user/Downloads")

'("/home/user/" "Downloads")

procedure

(dirname pth) → string?

  pth : path-string?

Example:
> (dirname "/home/user/Downloads")

"/home/user/"

procedure

(basename pth) → string?

  pth : path-string?

Example:
> (basename "/home/user/Downloads")

"Downloads"
