Casemate

Johan Persson (jzp) <johan at 2130 dot se>

1 Synopsis

casemate is a utility library for converting strings, symbols and byte-strings to various populat case-styles. It’s modeled after the excellent Clojure library camel-snake-kebab.

2 Reference

 (require casemate) package: casemate

Each of the following procedures take a string-like piece of data and converts it to the corresponding style.
