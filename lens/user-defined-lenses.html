1.3 Lenses on User-Defined Datatypes

In addition to the built-in lenses, lens provides utilities for construcing new lenses for user-defined datatypes. This section covers the utilities for creating lenses for Racket structs, as well as the general lens constructors for making arbitrary lenses.
