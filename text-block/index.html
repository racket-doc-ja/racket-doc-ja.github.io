Text block

Laurent Orseau

 (require text-block) package: text-block

Note: In DrRacket, to avoid intermediate blank pixel lines, uncheck Edit|Preferences…|Editing|General Editing|Add one pixel of extra space between lines.

Note: The package text-table can also be used in combination to text-block to produce unicode tables.
